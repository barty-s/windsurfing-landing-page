WINDSURFING

A sport for people of all ages and abilities!

Invented in California in the late 1960s by Jim Drake, windsurfing is a wind propelled water sport that is a combination of sailing and surfing. Windsurfing is a recreational, family friendly sport, most popular at flat water locations around the world that offer safety and accessibility for beginner and intermediate participants.
